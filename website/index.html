About the founder

Founder-led, technical, and mission-aware.

Federal DataWorks is led by M.A. Syed, a data and analytics practitioner focused on practical solutions for government, healthcare, finance, research, and public-sector operations. The company is new, but its delivery approach is grounded in hands-on technical interest and multi-year personal experience across analytics, dashboards, data engineering, automation, and AI-enabled workflows.

The firm is intentionally boutique: small enough to be responsive, technical enough to build, and structured to partner with federal buyers and prime contractors that need specialized data capability.

  • Technical focus: Python, SQL, Power BI, Tableau, Databricks, Azure, AWS, and Airflow
  • Domain interests: federal missions, healthcare, finance, research, data analytics, and modernization
  • Delivery style: clear communication, reproducible analysis, human-centered dashboards, and responsible AI
